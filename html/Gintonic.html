Gin Tonic

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • 60ml Gin
  • 20ml Tonic Water
  • Lime Wedges (for garnish)
  • Optional: Fresh herbs like mint or rosemary for extra aroma

Steps

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice cubes.
  2. Pour 60ml of gin over the ice.
  3. Add 120ml of tonic water to the glass.
  4. Gently stir the mixture with a bar spoon.
  5. Garnish with a lime wedge on the rim of the glass.
  6. Optionally, add a sprig of fresh herbs like mint or rosemary for a fragrant touch.
