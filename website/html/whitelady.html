White Lady

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • 45ml Gin
  • 25ml Cointreau (or other orange liqueur)
  • 0ml Fresh Lemon Juice
  • Optional: 1 Egg White (for a smooth texture)

Steps

  1. Add 45ml of gin to the shaker.
  2. Add 25ml of Cointreau (or other orange liqueur).
  3. Add 20ml of fresh lemon juice.
  4. If using egg white, add it to the shaker for a smooth texture.
  5. Add ice to the shaker and shake well for about 20 seconds.
  6. Strain the mixture into a chilled cocktail glass.
  7. Garnish with a lemon twist or a cherry (optional).
